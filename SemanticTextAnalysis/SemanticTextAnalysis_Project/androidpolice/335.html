[Update: Everything's Back] Google Drive, Docs, Sheets, Slides, And Keep Are Currently Down For Many

    Okay, everyone. The App Status Dashboard is giving us the all clear. Since around 3PM PT yesterday, all of Google's services have been back in working order.

Uh oh. Many users signing into Google Drive on the web are running into a little setback. The site loads, but that's it. Instead of a list of files, we're seeing a loading wheel that spins forever.

The problem is also affecting mobile apps.

This happens from time to time. Outages are an inherent risk of cloud services, and it's phenomenal that Google manages the uptime that it does. That said, even a momentary lack of access can be infuriating.

This issue is currently affecting Drive, Docs, Sheets, and Slides.

Update 1: 2015/10/09 1:17pm PDT

You can add Google Keep to the list as well.

End of Update

Google is aware of what's going on and has already updated its App Status Dashboard. Hopefully things smooth out soon.

Update 2: 2015/10/10 7:30am PDT

Okay, everyone. The App Status Dashboard is giving us the all clear. Since around 3PM PT yesterday, all of Google's services have been back in working order.

End of Update
